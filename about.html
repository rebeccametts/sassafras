About


Meet Darcy

Hi, I'm Darcy. I'm the photographer behind the lens at Sassafras Photography. Why Sassafras? I have had an interesting career life. It started with teaching middle school, then full-time motherhood, and now Photography. One of the BIGGEST compliments I have received often from my clients old and young is how fun my sessions are. Perhaps it's my experience as a teacher, but rarely have I run into a situation that hasn't been full of real laughter and lightheartedness. To me, this is critical in order to capture you and your loved ones in the most natural way possible. Kids have begged me to keep going...I think that speaks volumes.

Thank you for considering Sassafras Photography to capture your special moments. It truly is a privilege!
