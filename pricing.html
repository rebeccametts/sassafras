Special Note about Weddings and Events


Planning a wedding or event is a huge undertaking. I realize that at the end of the day, the person in charge of keeping this sacred event for history is the photographer. Therefore, I like to meet in person with you, see the venue, talk details together in order to come up with the best package that fits your needs. Event/Bridal consultations are $100, and can be applied to cost should you decide to book my services.


What's Included?

▸    Family, Children, and Senior Sessions include 25 edited proofs
▸    Professional Portrait (Headshot) Sessions include 10 edited proofs
▸    Weddings & Events (up to 150 guests) include 500 images
▸    Artistic hand editing of each image presented in your gallery

What to Expect?

▸    A one-hour photo shoot with as many outfit changes as you like
▸    Family sessions include a picnic, e.g. snacks, wine, doggie bones
▸    As we live in Colorado and I do not have a studio by choice, we all need to adapt to the ever changing weather patterns. The show goes on UNLESS it is raining (snow ok--no blizzards) or winds exceeding 25mph. I do frequent shoots inside peoples' homes or we can find an indoor area that is suitable (Union Station, for example). But please note that super bright sunny days aren't always the best for photos. Don't panic if you see clouds rolling in.

Cancellation Policy

You will be able to reschedule for free once, because everyone has things come up. Otherwise, a $75 cancellation fee will be assessed. However, should you cancel 48 hours or less before the session, a 50% session fee will be assessed.

Travel Fees

I am pretty much a homebody..that said, if you would like to hire my services outside of the Boulder Metro area, I charge a travel fee based on drive time/mileage. I prefer to do my sessions in this area because I know it well and, quite frankly, it's hard to find better backdrops anywhere.
